Today's speaker is Andrew Roger

Andrew received his B.Sc from the University of British Columbia and his PhD from Dalhousie University working with Ford Doolittle. He is a professor at Dalhousie University, and  was elected as a fellow of the Royal Society of Canada in 2012 for his work on eukaryotic superkingdoms, his work on the evolution of mitochondrion-related organelles in anaerobic protists and his contribution to investigating and improving phylogenetic models.
